About

I am a Turkologist and historical linguist affiliated with the Göttingen Academy of Sciences and Humanities. My research focuses on Old Turkic and Old Uyghur philology, Turkic–Mongolic language contact, historical linguistics, the Avaric language, and runiform inscriptions.

Research Interests

  • Old Turkic
  • Old Uyghur
  • Turkic–Mongolic Contact
  • Historical Linguistics
  • Avaric Studies
  • Runiform Inscriptions
